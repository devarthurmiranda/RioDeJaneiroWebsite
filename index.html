Maracanã Tour

Maracanã Stadium was the scene of great moments in Brazilian and world football, such as Pelé's 1,000th goal, the finals of the Campeonato Brasileiro and the first FIFA Club World Cup. It's also the largest stadium in Brazil.

Bondinho Park

In the heart of the Atlantic Forest, and above Rio de Janeiro, the Bondinho Park Pão de Açúcar enchants everyone and goes far beyond the stunning landscape.

Carnaval Experience

The Carnival Experience tourism program came up with the idea of presenting the universe of samba schools in Rio de Janeiro, showing the backstage of the creation of the show, the history of the birth of samba and Rio's carnival.
